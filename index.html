Are you struggling to book tee times for next golf outing? Are the websites you are using too difficult to use and causing you a lot of frustrations?

The solution is here. First Tee is an exciting, new innovative app that allows you to book tee times at your favourite courses faster than ever before.

Why use it?

As a golfer, if I’m looking to book a tee time I have to go through the process of ringing the club to do so. This isn’t as easy as it sounds because a lot of the time they don’t answer or don’t have the time you are looking for on that day. I preferably try to use their website because I can see all the times and what’s available. However, if you are unfamiliar with their website it can be very difficult to navigate and find where you can book a time. It can cause a lot of frustrations and waste a lot of time that we don’t have. First Tee address these problems in an easy to follow app.

How does it work?

Choose a course

You can select from a wide range of courses from all around the world. From local courses in your area to championship courses such as Royal County Down allowing you to play the same courses as your favourite golfers.

Pick a time

Whether it is for your next golf outing or a golf trip abroad with your friends. Booking tee times have never been easier. Once you have selected a course, you can see what tee times are available and how many spaces are left. Choose your desire tee time and number of players, then confirm. It’s as simple as that.

What's your favourite courses?

First Tee allows you to browse courses and favourite them for future booking. Tap on the heart icon and the course is saved away.

Let's play

The app is free to use and will keep track of all your bookings. This means less time wastes on booking a courses and more time playing them.
